{% translate "About Us" %}

{% blocktrans %} The application offers a convenient and user-friendly solution for discovering and participating in sports events and joining local sports communities. It aims to encourage users to lead an active lifestyle and discover new sports activities while meeting others with similar interests. The main features of the application include helping organisers to add clubs and shops, and creating sports events. However, the organisers must have a club or shop in order to benefit from event creation. Each organiser only has access to create one club. Visitors have the possibility to participate in sports events created by the organisers and to discover new sports activities by joining local clubs. They can also access the online shop to find equipment for their favourite sports activities. {% endblocktrans %}
